    Information Collection and Use:

  • No Personal Information: Our app does not collect any personal information from users. It operates using your iCloud account for functionality.

    Data Storage and Sharing:

  • Journal Entries: Our app allows users to record their emotions in their personal journals. These journal entries are stored securely within your iCloud account. We do not have access to or store any of this data on our servers.
  • Sharing with Other Users: Users have the option to share their journal entries with other users. It is the responsibility of the user to control the sharing of their data. We do not facilitate or have access to any shared journal entries.

    Data Security:

  • App Infrastructure: Our app utilizes Apple's Core Data and CloudKit technologies to securely store and manage user data. These technologies provide robust data protection measures to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your information.

    User Control and Choices:

  • Deleting Data: Users have full control over their data within the app. You can delete your journal entries and journals at any time. Deleted data is immediately removed from your iCloud account. Unsharing Journals: If you have shared a journal with another user, you have the ability to unshare it at any time, removing access to your shared data.
